    ALL IS ALL Verified

    Trust must be earned with evidence.

    ALL IS ALL Verified is an evidence-based assessment framework for evaluating AI systems across performance, reliability, security, governance, transparency, and measurable business value.

    LEVEL 1

    Observed

    The system is fully inventoried and its activity, cost, and permissions are continuously tracked.

    LEVEL 2

    Verified

    Performance, outcomes, and operational claims are validated against auditable evidence over time.

    LEVEL 3

    Trusted

    The system sustains verified performance, security posture, oversight, and value under continuous assessment.

    A proprietary, evidence-based trust standard — not a government or statutory certification.
