Mission

Complete visibility, control, evidence, and trust — for every organization's entire AI ecosystem.

The future is not one AI product.

The future is every organization operating thousands of intelligent systems — agents that act, models that decide, workflows that execute without waiting for a human.

Employees have identity, permissions, managers, payroll, compliance rules, and audit trails. The digital workforce has almost none of this. That gap is the defining infrastructure problem of the next decade.

ALL IS AI exists to make that future visible, measurable, secure, accountable, and trusted. ALL IS ALL is the platform that turns this mission into infrastructure.

What We Believe

Principles we build by.

Evidence over claims

Trust in AI will not be granted by marketing. It will be earned by verifiable records of what systems actually do.

Control is enablement

Organizations that can see and govern their AI scale it faster — not slower. Control is how ambition becomes safe to execute.

Independence matters

The layer that measures AI cannot belong to the vendors being measured. We are independent by design.

Honesty compounds

We do not invent customers, statistics, or certifications. What we publish is what is true — and that policy is permanent.
